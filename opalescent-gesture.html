    • Opalescent Gesture

    This video was a documentation of a gestural object I created out of cellophane and wire. The object comprised of two pieces dancing around each other, and changed throughout the day, its appearance dictated by the quality of the light coming through the window. Much of the video was shot at sunrise in order to capture the essence of the light. At night, the object turned into a canvas for firework-like lights, reflecting and absorbing the projected patterns.
