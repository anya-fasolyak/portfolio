    • America the Beautiful

    Inspired by the prompt "Now and Then," America the Beautiful is a comparison of the Civil Rights movement and the Black Lives Matter movement. The booklet contains articles from the web about the Black Lives Matter movement, and features collaged images taken from archives of the Civil Rights Movement alongside modern-day protestors.


    8.5" x 10.5", 44 pages.
    Printed on Mohawk Via Vellum 100T.
    Saddle-stitch binding.
