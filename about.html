    About Me

    I’m a thoughtful and empathetic designer who strives to think about people as humans, not users. In my professional work, I design user interfaces that aim at simplifying technological experiences. My work occurs at the intersection of human experiences and company stipulations, and I’m always working to mediate between the two.

    I’m always thinking about the bigger picture, and I firmly believe in design as a vehicle for societal change. My personal work strives to educate others and alter perspectives. I love experimentation, and believe that the most interesting results occur at the intersection of design and technology.

    I communicate both in English and in Russian. When I’m not designing, you can find me reading, gardening, or escaping into the great outdoors. On my lower energy days, I’m probably inside sketching or watching cartoons.
