    • Love Is Illegal

    Inspired by a This American Life podcast episode, this manuscript is a transcription of stories collected from the web of LGBTQ folk living in countries where being gay is against the law. The tome serves both as a sculptural object and as a bastardization of a legal document that mimics the censorship of individuals in homophobic countries.


    8" x 10", 356 pages.
    Printed on Mohawk Via Satin 100T.
    Hand-stitched binding.
